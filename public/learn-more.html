  • Reality: Buyers typically finance the commission through their mortgage. HomePilot helps you understand these hidden costs.

  • Reality: Commissions are negotiable by law — HomePilot shows you how to explore flat fees or negotiate rates.

  • Reality: Many agents discourage or ignore offers from unrepresented buyers. HomePilot helps you navigate around those gatekeepers with tools that put you in control.

Behind-the-Scenes: Steering and Incentives

Many agents steer clients toward homes with higher commissions and discourage offers where commission is reduced. For example, on a $500,000 home, 3% equals $15,000 — often financed through your mortgage, costing you even more over time.
