Although real estate rules are changing after 2024, buyers still face steering, pressure, and commission confusion.

Common Myths vs Reality

  • Myth: I don't need to worry about commissions because the seller covers them.
    Reality: Today, buyers must often pay their agents directly, and commissions are part of the offer.
  • Myth: Commissions are negotiable, so it’s easy to get a better deal.
    Reality: While commissions are negotiable, strong pressure keeps them close to traditional levels.
  • Myth: I'll be treated the same whether or not I have an agent.
    Reality: Some sellers and agents still favor buyers who offer full commissions.
